Serene Beaches

Indian beaches are famous all over the world and thousands of beach lovers arrive here from all corners of the world. After all, the long coastline is beautiful and lined with lovely beaches. It is very difficult for beach lovers to resist the waves, the cool breeze from the sea and the gorgeous views of the horizon. There are many beach destinations that are popular amongst tourists, such as Goa, Kerala, Puri, etc. But here too, there are many beaches that lie unexplored, waiting to be discovered. These beaches are not over-crowded by tourists and are perfect places to relax and enjoy some solitude.

  1. Radhanagar Beach, Andaman & Nicobar Islands

    Radhanagar Beach is one of the most famous attractions in Havelock Island and the Andaman and Nicobar Islands itself. Needless to say, it is one of the most spectacular beaches you will visit on your trip to Andaman. Hugged with Coconut trees from all sides and covered with tropical jungle from both the sides, the Radhanagar Beach opens up a world of complete serenity and relaxation for its visitors.

    Best time to visit: November to February

  2. Marari Beach, Kerala

    The Marari beach is one of the best in the whole of Kerala, with its clean white sands. The waters are clean and safe. Coconut trees line this long, scenic beach with white sand, waves & an adjacent lagoon.

    Best time to visit: September to May
